Lists of Nutrient Requirements

Balance Raw will list out either AAFCO, NRC, or FEDIAF nutrient requirements, differentiated by dog or cat, puppy or adult.
You can freely choose which requirement guideline you'd like to follow.

When your recipe has fulfilled the chosen requirement, it will turn green. 
A few of the requirements have a maximum intake, if your recipe exceeds them, they will turn red.


Dogs & Cats have different requirements.
