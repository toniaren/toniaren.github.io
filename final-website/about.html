How Raw Feeding Has Changed Us

#1

Better Fur and Coat

You pets are likely to shed less, experience less skin problems, and have shinier coats.

#2

Clean Teeth and No Plaque

Tartar and plaque builds more frequently in pets when kibble fed. Eating raw helps reduce this possibility and gives better oral health.

#3

Healthier Weight

Fresh food can help maintain a proper weight for your pets. Keeping a fit body shape means a longer longevity.

#4

Higher Energy

The high nutrition in raw feeding makes pets more energised and leads to better focus in training.
