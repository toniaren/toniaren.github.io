Bearded Dragon Care Guide

This is a simple guide for caring bearded dragons as pets.
Reptiles are cold-blooded animals and thus require a special husbandry.
Always do your own researches.

The Central Bearded Dragon (Pogona Vitticeps) is found throughout most
of Southern Australia.
Bearded Dragon has average captivity lifespan of 8-10 years.
And an average length, nose to tail-tip of between 18 and 22 inches.
