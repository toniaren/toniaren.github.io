The early adopters for this problem will be pet parents who already acknowledges the benefits of homemade diets and may or may not be already making homemade diets themselves, whereas there are currently no reliable resources for them to calculate if their homemade diets are balanced for their pets.
Complete and balanced raw pet food is also profoundly limited in mainland China, which are also a potential need for many raw-feeding families.

WHO: Pet parents interested in homemade pet foods
WHAT: Create a platform to act as a balancing calculator for their customised meals
WHY: No reliable resources to ensure balance in homemade diets currently
